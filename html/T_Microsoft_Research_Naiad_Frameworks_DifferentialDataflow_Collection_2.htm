Remarks

            The TTime type parameter is used to ensure that collections are
            combined in compatible ways, and need not be manipulated directly by the programmer. Initially,
            InputCollection TRecord  collections have an integer-valued Epoch timestamp,
            which indicates that they vary according to a stream of input epochs. The EnterLoop(LoopContext TTime ) method
            is used to refer to a collection within a loop, by augmenting its timestamp to have an IterationIn TTime  value
            with an additional loop counter.
